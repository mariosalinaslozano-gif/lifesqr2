About LifeQR Memorial QR Codes

A modern way to honor and remember loved ones through digital memorial pages accessible by QR code.

What is LifeQR?

Our QR code products allow you to create lasting tributes that can be placed on headstones, memorial plaques, or kept as personal keepsakes. When scanned, these codes link to personalized memorial pages where you can share photos, stories, and memories.

How Does It Work?

1. Purchase a Product

Choose from plaques, tags, stickers, or other memorial products with QR codes.

2. Create Memorial Page

Build a personalized digital memorial with photos, stories, and important dates.

3. Receive Your Product

Get your custom QR code product delivered to your door.

4. Share & Remember

Place the QR code and let others scan to view the memorial anytime.

Benefits of Digital Memorials

  • Accessible from anywhere with a smartphone
  • Share unlimited photos and videos
  • Update content anytime without changing the QR code
  • Privacy controls to manage who can view
  • Allow visitors to leave condolences and memories
  • Connect family and friends across distances

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the QR codes last?

Our QR codes are permanent and will work indefinitely. The memorial page is hosted as long as you maintain your account.

Can I update the memorial page after purchase?

Yes! You can update photos, text, and content anytime through your account dashboard.

Are the products weatherproof?

Yes, our outdoor products are designed to withstand weather conditions and UV exposure.
